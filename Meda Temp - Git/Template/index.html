The Digital Currency

Affordability, simplicity, and accessibility, MedaPort's digital currency eases the burden of saving fiat for medical purposes. Regardless of credit, age, background or location within the U.S., MedaPort gives all the chance to monitor, save and send money in a secure manner.

  • Learn more

Benefits

Send money to a licensed and verified practice without compromising payment information or risk exposure to the many security vulnerabilities within health administrative systems. An efficient method for saving money and using it specifically for medical purposes teaches users money management skills while also introducing them to a new and innovative form of technology.

Identity Management

MedaPort will implent the highest security measures to protect our users' accounts by implementing 2FA and PIN. Complying with local and federal KYC standards.

How it works?

Thanks to the open source powers of Bitcoin and Litecoin, we'll reuse the source code from an established blockchain and construct customized parameters in order to improve user experience, promote community involvement, increase acceptance among merchants( licensed physicians' offices) with added APIs and efficient payment processors, and make the benefits of the digital currency well known.

The Code

Build on Litecoin's establsihed blockchain using C++.

Security

2FA and biometrics.

Metrics

Locally scaled. Users able to send money to authorized public keys.

Device

Responsive web and mobile app.

The Blockchain

Public distributed ledger. Simplicity in reviewing and tracking previous transactions.

Inclusion

Anyone with internet access within the United States could purchase and distribute the currency accordingly.
